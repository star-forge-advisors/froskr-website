Why Froskr

Built on what we learned protecting legal teams at Amazon and Meta.

Before Froskr, we built compliance programs for legal teams inside two of the most heavily regulated technology companies in the world — programs that survived federal audits, managed cross-border data obligations, and protected attorney-client privilege at scale.

We started Froskr because small law firms carry the same ethical and regulatory obligations — ABA Model Rules, state bar cybersecurity guidance, client confidentiality requirements — but without dedicated security teams to enforce them.

Froskr exists to close that gap. We treat your compliance posture as core infrastructure: assessed, architected, and maintained continuously. Not a checkbox exercise. Not a one-time project. A program that evolves with your practice and the regulatory landscape.
